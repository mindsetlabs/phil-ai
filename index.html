Phil's response

Depression has a way of making the world feel heavy and fixed — like your current state is the final truth instead of a passing weather system.

Don't try to solve your whole life tonight. Just stay close to reality. Have water. Shower. Sit outside for ten minutes. Message one safe person. Tiny actions matter because they interrupt the spiral.

And be careful with the stories your mind is telling right now. A depressed mind speaks with certainty about hopelessness the same way an anxious mind speaks with certainty about danger. Neither are always reliable.
